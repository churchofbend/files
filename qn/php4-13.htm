Philippians 4:13

I can do all things through him who strengthens me.  ESV

“All things” is qualified by the preceding context of Paul’s contentment in times of hunger and abundance. This refers to circumstances beyond Paul’s control, and he bore them through the spiritual strength God provides. This does not teach that we are enabled by God to do anything we choose or desire to do.

~ SR
