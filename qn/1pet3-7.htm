1 Peter 3:7

Likewise, husbands, live with your wives in an understanding way, showing honor to the woman as the weaker vessel, since they are heirs with you of the grace of life, so that your prayers may not be hindered.  ESV

The weaker vessel analogy here finds its best parallel in what we might call our “fine China.” We may toss our everyday dishes in the sink or dishwasher with only moderate care, but we use extra care for more valuable, special dishware. This is a metaphor for how husbands should treat their wives, not for the physical strength or authority of women.

~ SR
