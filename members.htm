About Us

Jesus said, “I will build my church” (Matthew 16:18), and Paul wrote, “Christ is the head of the church, his body, and is himself its Savior” (Ephesians 5:23). Our goal is to be that church that Jesus built by following the instructions in God’s word.

We meet every Sunday to remember the Lord’s sacrifice, sing, pray, study, and give. We also meet on Wednesday evenings to sing, pray, and study. The first Wednesday of each month is dedicated to singing.

We’d love to see you at one of our assemblies, or you can contact us through this site, email, or Facebook Messenger. We’re happy to answer your questions about God’s gift of salvation and the church that meets here.
